SmartOne Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SmartOne ("the app", "we") handles information when you use our mobile application. Your privacy matters to us.

1. Information we DO NOT collect

SmartOne is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal information. Specifically:

  • No registration or account is required to use the basic features.
  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not sell data to third parties.

2. Data stored on your device

SmartOne stores certain information only on your device, never on external servers:

  • History: The JSON files you validate and your conversations are saved locally on your phone.
  • Preferences: Your app settings (font size, auto-save).

This data stays on your device and you can delete it at any time from Settings → Clear history, or by uninstalling the app.

3. AI Processing

When you use the artificial intelligence features (AI Chat, Analyze with AI, Performance Analysis), the text you send is securely processed through our server and the Anthropic API (Claude) to generate responses.

  • The content you send is transmitted encrypted (HTTPS).
  • An anonymous device identifier is used solely to apply daily usage limits. This identifier is not linked to your personal identity.
  • We recommend not sending sensitive or confidential information (passwords, personal data, private keys) through the AI features.

AI processing is also governed by Anthropic's privacy policies, available at anthropic.com/legal/privacy.

4. App permissions

SmartOne requests the following permissions:

  • Internet: Required for the artificial intelligence features.
  • Storage (optional): Only if you choose to load a JSON file from your device.

5. Security

Communications with our servers are made through encrypted connections (HTTPS). AI service access keys are handled securely on our server and are never stored on your device.

6. Children

SmartOne is a tool for developers and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page with a new update date.
